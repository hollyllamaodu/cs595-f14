   Rebecca Saxe:
   How we read each other's minds
   TEDGlobal 2009 · 16:51 · Filmed Jul 2009
   Subtitles available in 32 languages

   Sensing the motives and feelings of others is a natural talent for
   humans. But how do we do it? Here, Rebecca Saxe shares fascinating lab
   work that uncovers how the brain thinks about other peoples' thoughts —
   and judges their actions.
     * [82]Interactive transcriptInteractive transcript

   [83]Rebecca Saxe
   Cognitive neuroscientist
   Rebecca Saxe studies how we think about other people's thoughts. At the
   Saxelab at MIT, she uses fMRI to identify what happens in our brains
   when we consider the motives, passions and beliefs of others. [84]Full
