First picture of the Galaxy S5 mini ( Galaxy S5 Dx )

   [16]Mounir
   5/08/2014
   [17]galaxy s5 mini [18]samsung galaxy S5 news [19]samsung galaxy S5
   rumors
   Is Samsung following a new naming scheme for their spin-off devices?
   The Galaxy S5 zoom was launched as the Galaxy K zoom, and now, an image
   of the Galaxy S5 mini taken from Samsung’s UK website suggests that the
   phone will be launched as the Galaxy S5 Dx.
   [INS: :INS]

   It certainly seems like a good move, as it would help reduce the
   disappointment many feel when buying these devices going on the fact
   that they’re named after the company’s flagship product, and also give
   these devices an identity of their own.

            Read: [20]Official Hands-on of the Samsung GALAXY S5

          [21]First picture of the Galaxy S5 mini ( Galaxy S5 Dx )

   Leaked by evleaks, the site listing in the image is for the SM-G800,
   which is the model number for the S5 mini. As you can see, the design
   is pretty similar to that of the Galaxy S5, though Samsung seems to
   have added a few sporty color choices. Also noticeable is the lack of a
   heart rate sensor at the back, which isn’t surprising since we’re
   looking at a mid-range phone.
   The leaked image doesn’t show any specs, but thanks to our insiders,
   [22]we are already aware that it will sport a 4.5-inch Super AMOLED
   720p display, a quad-core processor, 1.5GB of RAM, 16GB storage, an
   8-megapixel camera, a 2,100 mAh battery, and Android 4.4 KitKat.
   [INS: :INS]

   If this is indeed the Galaxy S5 mini we’re looking at, then it suggests
   that an official announcement could be made very soon, though details
   on exact launch dates and pricing continue to elude us.
