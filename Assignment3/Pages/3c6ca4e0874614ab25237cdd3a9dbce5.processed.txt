Obamacare Facts

   Thanks to Obamacare, Americans now have access to quality, affordable
   health coverage that will be there when they need it.

Are you covered?

   Thanks to the Affordable Care Act, you now have basic rights and
   protections when it comes to insurance—there are now standards for
   quality health coverage.
