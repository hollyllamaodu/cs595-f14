	<h3 class="entry-title">
			Oracle Technology Network at JavaOne and OpenWorld 
	    </h3>

    <h4 class="entry-meta">By Yolande Poirier on <a href="#">Sep 03, 2014</a></h4>

    <div class="entry-body">
                                        	<p><i>By guest blogger Cassandra Clark </i> </p> 
  <p>It is September again! The OTN team is working hard to provide an excellent experience for the OTN members once again at <a href="https://www.oracle.com/javaone/index.html">JavaOne</a>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<a href="https://www.oracle.com/openworld/index.html">Oracle OpenWorld</a>&nbsp;in San Francisco, September 28th through October 2nd 2014. OTN events to put on your calendar right away!&nbsp;</p> 
  <ul> 
    <li>OTN Kick Off Event –&nbsp; <b>Sunday, September 28th, 3 to 5pm</b> - Perfect way to start the week!&nbsp; Join us for a beverage, grab a community wristband to help find like minded folks, and to network! In OTN Lounge Moscone South Upper Lobby. &nbsp;</li> 
  </ul> 
  <ul> 
    <li><a href="https://www.oracle.com/openworld/tech-fest/index.html" target="_blank">Oracle Technology Network's TechFest</a><b> - Monday, September 29th, 7pm to 9pm - </b>Developers, DBAs, Architects, Sys Admins and tech enthusiasts: come celebrate in Howard Street Plaza!</li> 
  </ul> 
  <ul> 
    <li><a href="https://blogs.oracle.com/usableapps/entry/dress_code_2_0_wearables" target="_blank">OTN Wearable Meetup </a>– <b>Tuesday, September 30th, 4 to 6pm</b> - Get your best wearables technology gear on and come hang out with the OTN Team and the <a href="http://www.oracle.com/usableapps" target="_blank">Oracle Applications User Experience</a> team.&nbsp; <b>Inexpensive yet tasteful gifts</b> for attendees sporting wearable tech, while supplies last!</li> 
  </ul> 
  <p>Once again the OTN team can be found in two locations; OTN Lounge in Moscone South and in <a href="https://www.oracle.com/javaone/javahub/index.html" target="_blank">Java Hub in Hilton</a>. Stay tuned to this blog to learn more details, but here are some of the highlights so far for both locations. &nbsp;</p> 
  <h3><font size="3"><a href="https://www.oracle.com/javaone/javahub/index.html" target="_blank">OTN Activities @ Java Hub / Hilton</a>&nbsp;-&nbsp;</font></h3> 
  <h3><font size="3">Daily Activities -</font></h3> 
  <ul> 
    <li>At the&nbsp;<a href="https://blogs.oracle.com/javaone/entry/hackergarten_at_javaone_and_others%20" target="_blank">Hackergarten</a>, join open-source contributors and start hacking away &nbsp;</li> 
    <li>At the 3D Modeling, you can create 3D objects with Java and watch the 3D printer do its magic&nbsp;</li> 
    <li>Meet The Experts offers you one-one conversations on the JCP, Java EE, Oracle university and more&nbsp;</li> 
    <li>Join the Scavenger Hunt &nbsp;and Get Your OTN Java Shirt!&nbsp;</li> 
  </ul> 
  <p><img width="256" height="221" src="https://blogs.oracle.com/otn/resource/javaonetshirtforblog.jpg" /> </p> 
  <h3><font size="3">OTN Lounge Moscone South Upper Lobby Activities and Meetups-</font></h3> 
  <h3><font size="3">Community Meetups -</font></h3> 
  <ul> 
    <li>RAC Attack – Sunday September 28th, 9am to 2pm <br /></li> 
    <li>Delphix and DbVisit – Monday September 29th, 3:30pm to 5pm <br /></li> 
    <li>Oracle Spatial and Graph users Meetup– Wednesday October 1st, 4 to 5pm</li> 
  </ul> 
  <h3>Daily Activities in OTN Lounge-</h3> 
  <ul> 
    <li>Make Your Own Shirt - some of the design options are below.</li> 
  </ul><img width="328" height="186" src="https://blogs.oracle.com/otn/resource/BaseDesigns%26Embellishments.jpg" /><br /> 
  <ul> 
    <li>Origami <br /></li> 
    <li>Photo Booth <br /></li> 
    <li>Kiosks featuring Oracle Community, ACE program and other community initiatives. </li> 
  </ul>
