   Vote for the NFL's top air and ground performers!
   One top passing performance can make a huge difference. So can one top
   rushing performance. And so can FedEx One Rate^SM -- simple, flat-rate

   FedEx Air & Ground® NFL Players of the Week. When the winners are

Le'Veon Bell, Pittsburgh Steelers

   Bell tallied 147 yards on 21 carries in the [29]Steelers' 37-19 victory

Knile Davis, Kansas City Chiefs

   34-15 victory against the [32]Miami Dolphins.
