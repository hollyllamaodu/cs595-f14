      <div class="article-block article-text-block  "><div class="st-text-align-"><p>We&rsquo;re counting down to Jerrod Carmichael&rsquo;s first HBO special, <em>Love at the Store</em>, with a mini-tour across the United States! Follow Jerrod on <a href="http://www.facebook.com/jerrodcarmichael" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Facebook</a> for a behind-the-scenes look at the tour, and watch the premiere October 4th at 10 p.m. on HBO.</p>

<p>We&rsquo;ll be giving away VIP tickets in each city, so be sure to follow along on <a href="http://www.facebook.com/jerrodcarmichael" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Facebook</a> and <a href="http://www.twitter.com/funnyordie" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Twitter</a>!</p>
</div>

</h2></div>

<li>9/25 - BROOKLYN - BABY&rsquo;S ALL RIGHT (<a href="http://www.ticketfly.com/purchase/event/688375?utm_medium=bks" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Tickets</a>)</li>
<li>9/26 - NEW YORK CITY - THE STAND (<a href="http://thestand.laughstub.com/event.cfm?id=341824&amp;amp;cart" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Tickets</a>)</li>
<li>9/28 - BOSTON - JOHNNY D&rsquo;S (<a href="http://thewilbur.com/johnny-d/artist/funny-or-die-jerrod-carmichael/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Tickets</a>)</li>
<li>9/29 - SAN FRANCISCO - PUNCH LINE (<a href="http://concerts.livenation.com/event/1C004D2FE5114E9F" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Tickets</a>)</li>
<li>9/30 - LOS ANGELES - THE COMEDY STORE - BELLY ROOM (<a href="http://bit.ly/1n3yjoU" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Tickets</a>)</li>
