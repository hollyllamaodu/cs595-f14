   Barry Schwartz:
   Our loss of wisdom
   TED2009 · 20:45 · Filmed Feb 2009
   Subtitles available in 37 languages
   [79]View interactive transcript

   Barry Schwartz makes a passionate call for "practical wisdom" as an
   antidote to a society gone mad with bureaucracy. He argues powerfully
   that rules often fail us, incentives often backfire, and practical,
   everyday wisdom will help rebuild our world.

   [88]Barry Schwartz
   Psychologist
   Barry Schwartz studies the link between economics and psychology,
   offering startling insights into modern life. Lately, working with Ken
   Sharpe, he's studying wisdom. [89]Full bio
