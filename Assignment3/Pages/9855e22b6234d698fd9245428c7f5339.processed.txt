[50]Dale Earnhardt Jr. Records Pick-Six With Ryan Kerrigan On Madden

   Posted by Stephen Czarda on September 24, 2014 – 9:36 am

   [51]Ryan Kerrigan, Matt Ryan

   Washington Redskins super fan and NASCAR driver Dale Earnhardt Jr. put
   his 32-man “Madden” league on notice last night, as he recorded a
   pick-six with linebacker Ryan Kerrigan in the league opener:

     32 friends in one league. First game tonight. [52]@RyanKerrigan91
     with dat pick6. [53]@xbox [54]@EAMaddenNFL
     [55]pic.twitter.com/5wjHj0tsDO

     — Dale Earnhardt Jr. (@DaleJr) [56]September 24, 2014

   While Kerrigan notched Junior a virtual touchdown at FedExField, the
   fourth-year Purdue product has logged two interception returns for
   touchdown at the home stadium before.
