Samsung Galaxy S5 manual now available online

   [15]Mounir
   4/02/2014
   [16]galaxy s5 [17]samsung galaxy S5 news
   The Galaxy S5 will be going on sale globally on April 11,
   [INS: :INS]

   and if you’re currently waiting to get your hands on one and would like
   to learn of all its features and functions beforehand, you can now
   download the manual for the device from Samsung’s website. The manual
   seems to be available in English, Dutch and French at the moment, and
   goes into details about how things work on the S5, while also
   explaining many of its important features, like the new S Health app,
   Download Booster, Ultra Power Saving Mode, and more.
