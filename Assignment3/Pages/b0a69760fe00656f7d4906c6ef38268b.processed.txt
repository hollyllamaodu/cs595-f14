   How to make work-life balance work
   TEDxSydney · 10:05 · Filmed May 2010

   Work-life balance, says Nigel Marsh, is too important to be left in the
   hands of your employer. Marsh lays out an ideal day balanced between
   family time, personal time and productivity — and offers some stirring
   encouragement to make it happen.
