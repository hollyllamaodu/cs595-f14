Bad title

   Jump to: [5]navigation, [6]search

   The requested page title is invalid. It may be empty, contain
   [7]unsupported characters, or include a non-local or incorrectly linked
   [8]interwiki prefix. You may be able to locate the desired page by
   searching for its name (with interwiki prefix, if any) in the search
   box.

   Possible causes are:
     * an attempt to load a [9]URL such as
       [10]http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/| (the | character is not
       permitted in page titles);
     * an attempt to load a URL pointing to a "non-local" interwiki page
       (usually those not run by the Wikimedia Foundation). For example,
       the URL [11]http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/meatball:WikiPedia will
       give this error, because the "meatball:" interwiki prefix is not
       marked as local in the interwiki table. Certain interwiki prefixes
       are marked as local in the table. For example, the URL
       [12]http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/meta:Main_page can be used to load
       [13]meta:Main_page. All interlanguage prefixes are marked as local,
       and thus URLs such as [14]http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/fr:Accueil
       will work as expected. However, non-local interwiki pages can still
       be accessed by interwiki linking or by entering them in the search
       box. For example [[meatball:WikiPedia]] can be used on a page, like
       this: [15]meatball:WikiPedia.

   If you tried to access a non-local interwiki page, you may be able to
   access that page by clicking the "article" tab on this page.
