Video of the Samsung Galaxy S5 ISOCELL camera sensors

   [15]Mounir
   3/12/2014
   [16]galaxy s5 [17]galaxy s5 specs
   The Galaxy S5 will be the first Samsung smartphone to come with a
   16-megapixel camera (well, unless you count the Galaxy S4 zoom a
   smartphone instead of a camera with a phone attached to it).
   [INS: :INS]

   And also debuting on the [18]S5 is Samsung’s ISOCELL camera sensor,
   which allows for better imaging especially in low-light conditions.

          [19]Video of the Samsung Galaxy S5 ISOCELL camera sensors

   Samsung has made a lot of noise about its ISOCELL technology, and now
   the company has released a new video that describes some of the
   advantages of the ISOCELL camera sensors.

            Watch: [20]Official Hands-on of the Samsung GALAXY S5

   The video is a bit technical, but you should be able to understand the
   advantages it offers anyway (though whether these translate into good
   real-life image capture on the Galaxy S5 compared to previous phones
   remains to be seen.) Go ahead and hit the play button below.
