   What's the next window into our universe?

   Big Data is everywhere — even the skies. In an informative talk,
   astronomer Andrew Connolly shows how large amounts of data are being
   collected about our universe, recording it in its ever-changing moods.
   Just how do scientists capture so many images at scale? It starts with
   a giant telescope …
