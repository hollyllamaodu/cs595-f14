   Ruth Chang:
   How to make hard choices

   Here's a talk that could literally change your life. Which career
   should I pursue? Should I break up — or get married?! Where should I
   live? Big decisions like these can be agonizingly difficult. But that's
   because we think about them the wrong way, says philosopher Ruth Chang.
   She offers a powerful new framework for shaping who we truly are.
