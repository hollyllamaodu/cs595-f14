			<div class="c61 c61v0">
				<div class="c61w1">
					<h1>
						Kids Get Creative with Computers</h1>
					<p>
						Teens can have fun and learn about programming, robotics, and engineering at the same time. Where? At Devoxx4Kids on September 27 in San Francisco, hosted at JavaOne.</p>
					<div class="obttn xxlbigbttn">
						<a class="graphicbutton" href="https://www.oracle.com/webfolder/s/delivery_production/docs/FY15h1/doc2/OES100328494-Featured-Workshops.html" target="_blank">Agenda</a> <a class="graphicbutton" href="http://eventreg.oracle.com/profile/web/index.cfm?PKWebId=0x1080922333" target="_blank">Register Now</a></div>
				</div>
			</div>
		</div>
		<div class="f10w3">
			<div class="c63 c63v0">
				<div class="c63w1">
					<div class="c63w2">
						<div class="c63w3 c63noshadow">
							<img alt="Devoxx4Kids Oracle A" src="//www.oracle.com/us/assets/c63-devoxx-oacademy-2245617.png" /></div>
					</div>
					<p>
						Do you know pre-teens and teens who want to have fun while learning more about programming, robotics, and engineering? Do you envision having coding duels with the next generation of programmers? If so, be sure they know about the Devoxx4Kids program taking place in conjunction with this year&#39;s JavaOne conference in San Francisco.</p>
					<p>
						JavaOne is offering a unique opportunity for 150 kids (ages 10 -18) to attend this exciting new Devoxx4kids program. The program will take place on Saturday, September 27 (before the official start to JavaOne on September 28).</p>
					<p>
						Topics covered should include:</p>
					<ul class="mondo-grey listicons">
						<li>
							Java</li>
						<li>
							Alice</li>
						<li>
							Arduino</li>
						<li>
							Greenfoot</li>
						<li>
							Lego Mindstorms</li>
						<li>
							Minecraft modding</li>
						<li>
							NAO Robot</li>
						<li>
							Python</li>
						<li>
							Raspberry Pi</li>
						<li>
							Scratch</li>
					</ul>
