Pictures of the premium Samsung Galaxy F leak

   [15]Mounir
   6/09/2014
   [16]galaxy s5 prime [17]Samsung Galaxy F
   The Samsung Galaxy F is expected to be the "premium" version of the
   Samsung Galaxy S5, with features that include a 5.3 inch 1440 x 2560
   QHD screen, a Qualcomm Snapdragon 805 processor, 3GB of RAM, and a 16MP
   rear camera with OIS.
   [INS: :INS]

   Instead of a plastic body, the Galaxy F employs a metal casing. What
   gives this rumor some bite, is that prior to the unveiling of the
   Samsung Galaxy S5 at MWC, there was speculation about a QHD screened
   Samsung Galaxy F.

     Read: [18]Samsung Galaxy S5 Prime and Active break cover on camera

   [19]Pictures of the premium Samsung Galaxy F leak

   One of our loyal readers has sent us a couple of pictures allegedly
   showing off a pre-production model of the Samsung Galaxy F. He tells us
   that this particular unit carries the Snapdragon 801 inside, although
   that will be changed to the Snapdragon 805 by the time the phone rolls
   off the assembly line. He also tells us that the phone is slightly
   smaller than the Samsung Galaxy S5 with thinner bezels. Oddly enough,
   he notes that the phone is "asymmetrical" with the top of the handset
   more curved. In case you were wondering, the Samsung Galaxy F is said
   to be IP 67 certified, giving the "premium" version of Samsung's
   flagship model the same protection against dust and water as the Galaxy
   S5.
   [INS: :INS]

         [20][Samsung-Galaxy-F2.jpg] [21][Samsung-Galaxy-F.jpg.png]

   As usual, we suggest that you take these images with a grain of salt.
   It is always healthy to be cynical to a certain degree when dealing
   with leaks.
