Samsung Galaxy F is a Premium Flagship With Metallic body

   [16]Mounir
   3/10/2014
   [17]Samsung Galaxy F [18]samsung galaxy S5 concept [19]samsung galaxy
   S5 rumors
   The Samsung Galaxy F series gets a new model today, courtesy of a
   concept created by Ivo Marić and shown below. The device is simply
   known as the Samsung Galaxy F and it measures 8.1 mm in thickness, just
   like the Galaxy S5.
   [INS: :INS]

        [20]Samsung Galaxy F is a Premium Flagship With Metallic body

   It has a different curvature of the corners and Home button and the
   side of the handset is chamfered, so you can pick it up easily. The
   Samsung Galaxy F features a physical camera button and another button
   that can get a function assigned to it. For example you assign to it
   the flash features, HDR On/Off and many others.

                Read: [21]Why a premium GALAXY S5 make sense
        [22]Samsung Galaxy F is a Premium Flagship With Metallic body
                                 [INS: :INS]

   The Galaxy F concept has a 5.2 inch screen and it’s a little bigger
   than the Galaxy S5. Its body is made of metal and the battery is
   removable. The back side is very much like the one of the Galaxy S5,
   particularly the camera area, that also seems to include a flash and
   heart sensor. I’m guessing what this body is also waterproof, since I
   see that the microUSB 3.0 port is sealed.

        [23]Samsung Galaxy F is a Premium Flagship With Metallic body

   In the end this feels like a metallic Galaxy S5 with some design
   changes. Do you like it?
