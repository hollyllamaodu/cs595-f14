                <div class="tools_tips toolCont">
                    <div class="header-container">
                        <h2 class="sectionTitle white">the tools</h2>
                    </div>
                    <div class="body-container">
                        <div class="tipsCont">

                            <div class="tip">
                                <h4>tip no. 1</h4>
                                <p>Talk to your friends honestly and openly about sexual assault.</p>
                            </div>

                            <div class="tip">
                                <h4>tip no. 2</h4>
                                <p>Don't just be a bystander - if you see something, intervene in any way you can.</p>
                            </div>

                            <div class="tip">
                                <h4>tip no. 3</h4>
                                <p>Trust your gut. If something looks like it might be a bad situation, it probably is.</p>
                            </div>

                            <div class="tip">
                                <h4>tip no. 4</h4>
                                <p>Be direct. Ask someone who looks like they may need help if they're okay.</p>
                            </div>

                            <div class="tip">
                                <h4>tip no. 5</h4>
                                <p>Get someone to help you if you see something - enlist a friend, RA, bartender, or host to help step in.</p>
                            </div>

                            <div class="tip">
                                <h4>tip no. 6</h4>
                                <p>Keep an eye on someone who has had too much to drink.</p>
                            </div>

                            <div class="tip">
                                <h4>tip no. 7</h4>
                                <p>If you see someone who is too intoxicated to consent, enlist their friends to help them leave safely.</p>
                            </div>

                            <div class="tip">
                                <h4>tip no. 8</h4>
                                <p>Recognize the potential danger of someone who talks about planning to target another person at a party.</p>
                            </div>

                            <div class="tip">
                                <h4>tip no. 9</h4>
                                <p>Be aware if someone is deliberately trying to intoxicate, isolate, or corner someone else.</p>
                            </div>

                            <div class="tip">
                                <h4>tip no. 10</h4>
                                <p>Get in the way by creating a distraction, drawing attention to the situation, or separating them.</p>
                            </div>

                            <div class="tip">
                                <h4>tip no. 11</h4>
                                <p>Understand that if someone does not or cannot consent to sex, it's rape.</p>
                            </div>

                            <div class="tip">
                                <h4>tip no. 12</h4>
                                <p class="sm">Never blame the victim.</p>
                            </div>

                            <div class="tip">
                                <h4>tip no. 13</h4>
                                <p class="lg">If you are a victim or survivor, or helping someone in that situation go to <a href="https://www.notalone.gov/" target="_blank">notalone.gov</a> to get the resources and information you need. You can also call the National Sexual Assault Hotline at 1.800.656.HOPE</p>
                            </div>
