   Hans Rosling:
   Insights on HIV, in stunning data visuals
   TED2009 · 10:02 · Filmed Feb 2009
   Subtitles available in 39 languages
   [81]View interactive transcript

   Hans Rosling unveils data visuals that untangle the complex risk
   factors of one of the world's deadliest (and most misunderstood)
   diseases: HIV. By following the data, he suggests a surprising key to
   ending the epidemic.
     * [89]Interactive transcriptInteractive transcript

   [90]Hans Rosling
   Global health expert; data visionary
   In Hans Rosling’s hands, data sings. Global trends in health and
   economics come to vivid life. And the big picture of global
   development—with some surprisingly good news—snaps into sharp focus.
