<p>According to the International Centre for Prison Studies, nearly a third of all female prisoners worldwide are incarcerated in the United States of America. There are 201,200 women in US prisons, representing 8.8 percent of the total American prison population.</p> 
<p>China comes a very distant second to the United States with 84,600 female prisoners in total or 5.1% of the overall Chinese prison population. Russia is in third position â€“ 59,000 of its prisoners are women and this comes to 7.8 percent of the total.</p> 
<p>Across the world, 625,000 women and children are being held in penal institutions with the female prison population growing on all five continents.</p>
