Washington Redskins Morning Clips: 9/23

   Posted Sep 23, 2014

   [Czarda_Stephen.JPG] Stephen Czarda Associate Writer [47]@Redskins
     * [48]a
     * [49]a

A morning roundup of what the local and national media have to say about the
Washington Redskins on Tuesday, Sept. 23, 2014.

   A morning roundup of what the local and national media have to say
   about the Washington Redskins on Tuesday, Sept. 23, 2014.

   All reports, rumors and conjecture are a reflection of the media
   conversation and are not endorsed or confirmed by the Washington
   Redskins.

   .

   -- It was announced on Monday that three-time Pro Bowl cornerback
   [50]DeAngelo Hall [icon-article-link.gif] was placed on season-ending
   injured reserve. [51]More on the impact of his loss via Redskins.com's
   Andrew Walker.

   – Desean Jackson’s return to Philadelphia saw him score one of his
   classic long touchdowns, [52]as he bolted past the Eagles’ defense for
   an 81-yard score during the third quarter of Sunday’s game.

   -- Quarterback [53]Kirk Cousins [icon-article-link.gif]  [54]dazzled in
   his first start of the season, as he threw for 427 yards and 3
   touchdowns. In the first quarter, he became the first Redskins
   quarterback since Mark Brunell to throw 2 touchdown passes in the first
   quarter of a game. Now, he's one of three nominees for Week 3 "FedEx
   Air Player Of The Week." [55]Vote for him here.

   -- The Redskins face a short week, as they’ll host the New York
   Giants [56]on Thursday at FedExField.

   -- Redskins Blog with more on running back [57]Alfred Morris
   [icon-article-link.gif] cracking the franchise top-10 in [58]career
   rushing yards.

   -- '[59]A Football Life: Sean Taylor' will air this Friday at 9 p.m. on
   NFL Network.

   -- CSN Washington’s Rich Tandler on some [60]possible players that will
   fill the open spot on the 53-man roster and cornerback [61]Chase
   Minnifield [icon-article-link.gif] [62]being signed to the active
   roster.

   -- ESPN’s John Keim with [63]five questions facing the Redskins before
   their Week 4 matchup with the New York Giants.

   -- The Washington Post with [64]unofficial snap count totals from the
   Redskins' Week 3 game.
