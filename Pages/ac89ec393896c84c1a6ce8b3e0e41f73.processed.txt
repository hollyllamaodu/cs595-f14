   In a spellbinding talk, paleontologist Jack Horner tells the story of
   how iconoclastic thinking revealed a shocking secret about some of our
   most beloved dinosaurs.
     * [77]Interactive transcriptInteractive transcript

   [78]Jack Horner
   Dinosaur digger
   Jack Horner and his dig teams have discovered the first evidence of
   parental care in dinosaurs, extensive nesting grounds, evidence of
   dinosaur herds, and the world’s first dinosaur embryos. He's now
   exploring how to build a dinosaur. [79]Full bio
