<section class="entry-content clearfix">
	<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-91911" src="http://tedconfblog.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/tedglobal-2014-books-1.jpg?w=900&#038;h=442" alt="TEDGlobal-2014-books-1" width="900" height="442" /></p>
<p>Can’t wait for <a href="http://conferences.ted.com/TEDGlobal2014/" target="_blank">TEDGlobal 2014</a>? We’re here to help! Spend the next two weeks curling up with these books by the wonderful <a href="http://blog.ted.com/2014/09/02/tedglobal-2014-the-full-lineup-revealed/" target="_blank">speakers</a> who will grace the stage in Rio.</p>
<h2>Memoir</h2>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0385319630/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=0385319630&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=KKH34IGG2SBOVO4O" target="_blank"><b><i>American Chica: Two Worlds, One Childhood</i></b><b>, by Marie Arana</b></a>. The writer&#8217;s classic, in which she shares her own experience growing up between Peru and the United States, which she describes as a “north-south collision.”</p>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/162779073X/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=162779073X&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=D3ECAXEJNZPGKSD2" target="_blank"><b><i>No Place to Hide</i></b><b>, by Glenn Greenwald</b></a>. In his new book, journalist Glenn Greenwald takes a detailed look at government snooping and tells the story of working with Edward Snowden to leak classified NSA documents.</p>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1401926630/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=1401926630&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=PSYSHKMHHDCWXGTY" target="_blank"><b><i>Why Meditate: Working with Thoughts and Emotions</i></b><b>, by Matthieu Ricard</b></a>. Ricard, now a monk but once a biochemist, presents a clear guide to how to meditate.</p>
<h2>Fascinating regions</h2>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1439110204/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=1439110204&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=OBBWAGGOWI6GLDER" target="_blank"><b><i>Bolivar</i></b><b>, by Marie Arana</b></a>. The biographer and critic’s latest book follows Simón Bolívar’s career and life, from his campaign to liberate Colombia and Venezuela to his love affairs.</p>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0393088588/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=0393088588&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=P6TJ7TAMNWKHWZBI" target="_blank"><b><i>Indonesia, Etc.: Exploring the Improbable Nation</i></b><b>, by Elizabeth Pisani.</b></a> Pisani’s new book explores Indonesia in all its richness and contradiction.</p>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0142422568/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=0142422568&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=AZUA2PCKTJYQYHHZ" target="_blank"><b><i>The Balkans: Nationalism, War and the Great Powers, 1804-2011</i></b><b>, by Misha Glenny</b></a>. Glenny offers a comprehensive look at the history of the Balkans.</p>
<h2>Big global problems</h2>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0821388266/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=0821388266&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=N76FWZOH7KNXKWBK" target="_blank"><b><i>Migration and Remittances During the Global Financial Crisis and Beyond</i></b><b>, edited by Dilip Ratha, Ibrahim Sirkeci and Jeffrey H. Cohen</b></a>. In their 2012 book, the authors investigate the impact of the financial crisis on migrants from developing countries.</p>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0393337650/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=0393337650&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=IHUQ7ZLG7DRSTIYK" target="_blank"><b>The Wisdom of Whores: Bureaucrats, Brothels and the Business of AIDS, by Elizabeth Pisani</b></a>. Wielding wit and political incorrectness, Pisani takes a bold look at how governments are reluctant to fund effective HIV prevention.</p>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1400095123/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=1400095123&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=SLBAQICQDEH5W3OE" target="_blank"><b><i>McMafia: A Journey Through the Global Criminal Underworld</i></b><b>, by Misha Glenny</b></a>. The journalist unleashes a shocking fact—that domestic trade accounts for an estimated one-fifth of the global gross domestic product. In a feat of reporting, he then takes us inside some of those illegal economies.</p>
<h2>Global stability</h2>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/027102920X/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=027102920X&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=G3BRJ2MIBCSKKA64" target="_blank"><b><i>Cops Across Borders</i></b><b>, by Ethan Nadelmann</b></a>. The drug policy reformer’s book, originally published in 1994, examines the United States’ role in international law enforcement.</p>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1107632048/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=1107632048&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=FMONMYCIYFOCDTJK" target="_blank"><b><i>Peaceland: Conflict Resolution and the Everyday Politics of International Intervention</i></b><b>, by Séverine Autesserre</b></a>. The political scientist’s book, which came out earlier this year, examines peacebuilding interventions.</p>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0415536766/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=0415536766&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=6XYE42I3AXQ4VMJL" target="_blank"><b><i>Stabilization Operations, Security and Development: States of Fragility</i></b><b>, edited by Robert Muggah</b></a>. Muggah, a specialist in security and development, offers a review of international stabilization efforts.</p>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1842775553/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=1842775553&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=YAIL34R6L74UT7SK" target="_blank"><b><i>Rights, Resources and the Politics of Accountability</i></b><b>, edited by Joanna Wheeler and Peter Newell</b></a>. This 2006 book examines ways in which marginalized groups mobilize to fight for their rights.</p>
<h2>Fiction</h2>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1594204497/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=1594204497&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=YVBAKEK3CH4VTLZD" target="_blank"><b><i>Ghana Must Go</i></b><b>, by Taiye Selasi</b></a>. This debut novel, published last year, follows a long-dispersed family as they gather in Ghana following their father’s death.</p>
<p><img class="size-full wp-image-91912 aligncenter" src="http://tedconfblog.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/tedglobal-books-2.jpg?w=900&#038;h=445" alt="TEDGlobal-books-2" width="900" height="445" /></p>
<h2>Art/Design/Architecture</h2>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/3832797599/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=3832797599&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=V32K2DH4MBDC74O5" target="_blank"><b><i>Before They Pass Away</i></b><b>, by Jimmy Nelson</b></a>. The photographer documents over 30 remote tribes in more than 500 images.</p>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/2330004575/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=2330004575&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=Z3ZJM5JDL3OFYJOF" target="_blank"><b><i>Vik Muniz: Le Musée Imaginaire</i></b><b>, by Vik Muniz and Eric Mézil</b></a>. This volume accompanied a show in which Muniz, an artist, responded to the Collection Lambert in Avignon.</p>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/4887063202/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=4887063202&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=PUD3AXYORMYAZ7GO" target="_blank"><b><i>The Forces in Architecture</i></b><b>, by Alejandro Aravena</b></a>. The architect takes a technical look at his field in a book designed for architecture students and professionals.</p>
<h2>Science</h2>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1118460618/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=1118460618&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=OA4VEOMBKUFKC32N" target="_blank"><b><i>Novel Plant Bioresources</i></b><b>, by Ameenah Gurib-Fakim</b></a>. A biodiversity scientist presents a comprehensive resource for understanding under-utilized plant species.</p>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1250002613/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=1250002613&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=BJZB2UOZQ52BKOGF" target="_blank"><b><i>Beyond Boundaries: The New Neuroscience of Connecting Brains with Machines—and How It Will Change Our Lives</i></b><b>, by Miguel Nicolelis</b></a>. The neuroscientist explores how the brain creates thought, and the role machines will play in this process.</p>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/052175576X/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=052175576X&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=R5DNY7H4FCZEA2NF" target="_blank"><b><i>Measuring and Modeling the Universe, Volume 2</i></b><b>, edited by Wendy L. Freedman</b></a>. The astronomer’s 2010 textbook covers theories on the evolution of the universe.</p>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0071636595/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=0071636595&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=IDBCMFQGZNCK5AGG" target="_blank"><b><i>Gastrointestinal Imaging Cases</i></b><b>, by Jorge A. Soto, Stephen Anderson and Christine Menias</b></a>. This 2013 textbook surveys more than 150 gastrointestinal cases.</p>
<h2>The environment</h2>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1405333081/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=1405333081&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=BFOOQQ2NRGVL6FHS" target="_blank"><b><i>Illustrated Encyclopedia of the Ocean</i></b><b>, edited by Fabien Cousteau</b></a>. This beautifully illustrated book dives into the ocean’s mysteries.</p>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0152024867/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=0152024867&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=LPSONYI7UHLPOVFK" target="_blank"><b><i>The Shaman’s Apprentice: A Tale of the Amazon Rainforest</i></b><b>, by Mark Plotkin and Lynne Cherry</b></a>. Plotkin and his coauthor focus on one boy’s quest to become a tribal shaman.</p>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0767931750/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=0767931750&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=N5LFXJJSWA6EL5RC" target="_blank"><b><i>Antarctica 2041: My Quest to Save the Earth’s Last Wilderness</i></b><b>, by Robert Swan</b></a>. Swan, a polar explorer and environmentalist, looks at the state of the earth in light of the looming deadline of 2041, when the treaty protecting Antarctica is up for review.</p>
<h2>Business and philanthropy</h2>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1118249747/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=1118249747&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=NRHGYISWMIFYJOLC" target="_blank"><b><i>Jugaad Innovation: Think Frugal, Be Flexible, Generate Breakthrough Growth</i></b><b>, by Navi Radjou, Jaideep Prabhu, and Simone Ahuja</b></a>. Radjou and his coauthors examine how to drive innovation in an increasingly unpredictable business landscape via <i>jugaad</i>, Hindi for a clever improvised solution.</p>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1596916958/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=1596916958&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=BSVPVY43BDHJNM3O" target="_blank"><b><i>Philanthrocapitalism: How Giving Can Save the World</i></b><b>, by Michael Green and Matthew Bishop</b></a>. The social progress expert and his coauthor look at the way billionaires are reshaping philanthropy.</p>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1591840260/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=1591840260&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=teco06-20&amp;linkId=G3MBZIVFUHMX6O5K" target="_blank"><b><i>The Seven-Day Weekend: Changing the Way Work Works</i></b><b>, by Ricardo Semler</b></a>. Semler argues for employee satisfaction over corporate goals in this classic book, published a decade ago.</p>
