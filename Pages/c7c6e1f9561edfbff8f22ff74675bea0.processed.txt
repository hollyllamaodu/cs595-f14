Samsung Galaxy S5 Prime To Come In 5 Colors

   [15]Mounir
   5/16/2014
   [16]galaxy s5 prime [17]Samsung Galaxy F
   The Samsung Galaxy S5 Prime is expected to be launched next month,
   earlier in the week we heard that the new high end Galaxy S5 will
   retail for around £650 in the UK without a contract.
   [INS: :INS]

   Now we have some more details on the Galaxy S5 Prime, the handset will
   launch in five colors, which will include black, white, gold, blue and
   pink.

                Read: [18]Why a premium GALAXY S5 make sense

               [19]Samsung Galaxy S5 Prime To Come In 5 Colors

   The Samsung Galaxy S5 Prime will get a number of upgrades over the
   existing S5, the device will feature a 5.1 inch QHD display that will
   have a resolution of 2560 x 1440 pixels.

   The processor on the S5 Prime will also be updated, and the device is
   said to feature a quad core 2.5GHz Qualcomm Snapdragon 805 processor
   and 3GB of RAM.

   The S5 Prime will also come with 32GB of built in storage, the device
   will feature a microSD card slot that supports uo to 128GB cards, and
   there is also front and rear facing cameras.

   On the front of the handset there will be the same 2.1 megapixel camera
   as the S5, on the back a 16 megapixel camera, and it will come with
   Samsung’s TouchWiz UI and Android 4.4 Kit Kat.
   [INS: :INS]

   One thing we have yet to heard about the new S5 Prime, is whether the
   exterior of the device will get updated, the Galaxy S5 comes with a
   plastic casing, lets hope Samsung chooses some high end materials for
   their Galaxy S5 Prime.

   As well as the Samsung Galaxy S5 Prime, we are also expecting Samsung
   to launch the new Galaxy S5 Mini in June, there is also a Samsung
   Galaxy S5 Active on the way, although as yet we are not sure if this
   handset will launch next month.

   As soon as we get some more details on the new Samsung Galaxy S5 Prime,
   including confirmation on the handsets specifications, and some actual
   pictures on the device, we will let you guys know.
