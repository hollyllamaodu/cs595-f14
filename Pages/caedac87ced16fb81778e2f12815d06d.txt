<h1 class="posthead">Welcome</h1>
<div class="postcontent">
<center><img src="./images/DSC_6169.jpg" alt="Group Shot 2007"></center><br>
<p>MOUNTAIN HOUNDS 2014 WILL
BE HELD May 29 &ndash; June 2, 2014</p>
<p>Mountain Hounds is always
held the weekend after Memorial Day. We do hope that you will make plans to
join us in Gatlinburg, TN. You can expect to meet new friends and catch up with
the old ones as you spend a great weekend in the Smoky Mountains of Tennessee
with your greyhound(s).</p>
<p>This will be the TENTH year for Mountain Hounds and the volunteer team is busy planning activities, speakers and fun. We welcome suggestions for speakers and seminars, if you know of someone please let us know. Or if your group would like to sponsor an event please contact us at <a href="mailto:gfncgreyhound@yahoo.com">gfncgreyhound@yahoo.com</a>. We would love to hear from you.</p>
<p>The Sidney James Mountain Lodge is our host hotel and they are taking reservations now. Be sure and mention Mountain Hounds for the room discount. If you need a first floor room make your reservation early. Check the <a href="http://gfncmountainhounds.com/accomodations.shtml">ACCOMODATIONS</a> page for contact information. Registration will kick off in January and information will be located under the <a href="http://gfncmountainhounds.com/registration.shtml">REGISTRATION</a> page. We look forward to seeing you in Gatlinburg for Mountain Hounds 2014!</p>
<p>Here is a link to photos from our 2013 event: <a href="http://s759.photobucket.com/user/MH_Santa/library/Mountain%20Hounds%202012#/user/MH_Santa/library/Mountain Hounds2013?sort=3&amp;page=1&amp;_suid=138082600489909317293067181505" target="_blank">Mountain Hounds 2013 Pictures</a>.</p>
<p>Also, be sure to follow us on Facebook for the latest updates.</p>
