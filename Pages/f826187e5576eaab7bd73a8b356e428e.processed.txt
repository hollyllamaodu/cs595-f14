   Misha Glenny:
   How global crime networks work
   TEDGlobal 2009 · 19:30 · Filmed Jul 2009
   Subtitles available in 30 languages
   [72]View interactive transcript
   Share this idea [73]Watch Later [74]Download [75]Rate [76]Share on

   Journalist Misha Glenny spent several years in a courageous
   investigation of organized crime networks worldwide, which have grown
   to an estimated 15% of the global economy. From the Russian mafia, to
   giant drug cartels, his sources include not just intelligence and law
   enforcement officials but criminal insiders.
