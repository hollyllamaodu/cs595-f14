How to disable S Voice on the Galaxy S5

   [16]Mounir
   5/06/2014
   [17]galaxy s5 [18]galaxy s5 tips [19]galaxy s5 tricks
   Samsung’s own “S Voice” personal assistant has improved a lot in the
   last few years. Still, many people Android’s built-in Google Now search
   or some other app from the Play Store.
   [INS: :INS]

   Luckily there is a way to disable S Voice completely, and also speed up
   your Galaxy S5 in the process. It’s very easy to do if you know what
   you’re looking for. We’ll show you how it’s done.

     Read: [20]How to Get Better Galaxy S5 Battery Life With Ultra Power
                                 Saving Mode

                 [21]How to disable S Voice on the Galaxy S5

   By default S Voice is launched with a double-tap on the home button.
   Not only does this make it easy to accidentally launch S Voice, but it
   also creates lag. When S Voice is enabled and you press the home button
   it pauses ever so briefly to wait for the second press. Disabling S
   Voice will remove the delay and take you to the homescreen instantly.
   [INS: :INS]
    1. Double tap the home button to open S Voice.
    2. Press the menu button in the top right of the app.
    3. Tap “Settings.”
    4. Uncheck “Open via the home key.”

   That’s it! You are now free from S Voice. This also works with other
   devices that come with S Voice.
